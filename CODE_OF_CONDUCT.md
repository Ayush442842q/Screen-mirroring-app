# Code of Conduct

MirrorLink follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Help keep discussions focused and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this standard.
